# Stepwise Project Planning

  • STEP 0 :-
      0. SELECT THE PROJECT :

    • Deciding whether th project can be taken or not
    • Technical , Organizational & Financial feasibility is considered

  • STEP 1 :-
      1. IDENTIFY PROJECT SCOPE & OBJECTIVE :

    • STEP 1.1 : Identify objective & practical measure of the effectiveness in meeting those objective.
    • STEP 1.2 : Establish objective authority to ensure the unity of purpose among all persons concerned .
    • STEP 1.3 : Identify all stakeholder in project & their intrests.
    • STEP 1.4 : Modify objective in the light of stakeholder analysis.
    • STEP 1.5 : Establish method of communication between all parties .
